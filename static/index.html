About this Web App

I often watch video tutorials. Then I would want to rewatch a certain part of a video about something that I forgot. Unfortunately, finding the right time I need to rewatch can be challenging. I often waste alot of time looking, forcing me to rewatch a greater portion of the video than I need to. That is why I came up with this web app. I can save video timestamps with a title and a bookmark that I can search and find at a later time. It's like taking bookmarks which you can then click on to replay the exact part of the video you need to rewatch.
